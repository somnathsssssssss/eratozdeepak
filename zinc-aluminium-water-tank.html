Zinc Aluminum Water Tanks:

We offer Premium Quality Zincalume / Zinc-Aluminum Pre-Engineered Water Storage Tanks for Industrial, Government, Rural and other Projects. As we make almost all parts of the tank in-house with state-of-art technology, our Zinc-Aluminum Water Tanks are of best quality as per international quality standards. We offer Tanks in wide range of sizes from 10,000 liters up to 50,00,000 liters as per customers' requirements and needs. We can make all different volumes and adjust the diameter/height customized as per customer’s requirements.


Zinc Aluminum Tanks / Zincalume Tanks / Galvalume Tanks / ZnAl Tanks are considered the most economical solution for large capacity storage of water.


We use premium quality food grade liners and our tanks are installed in many government drinking water supply projects.


All our manufactured Zincalume Tanks are compliant with IS 15961 standard. We source the main steel directly by long term contracts with Tata Bluescope. We have modern facility to develop all the vital parts of tanks in our manufacturing unit which gives us leverage to maintain the high quality check on the final product.


Zincalume / Zinc-Aluminum are an alloy consisting of 55% Aluminium, 43.5% Zinc (by weight) and 1.5% Silicon. It is special alloy which has been developed after years of research internationally. Steel when coated with Zincalume lasts longer because it gets the Barrier Protection of Aluminum and Sacrificial Properties of Zinc. The product is made of high tensile / strength steel, which after special pre-treatment is coated with Zincalume/Galvalume alloys having metals like Aluminium (55%) which pro­vides barrier protection to steel and Zinc (43.5%) which has sacrificial properties.
