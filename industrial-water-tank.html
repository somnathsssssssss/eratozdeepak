Industrial Water Tanks

We make two types of industrial water tanks which are most practical and of latest technology. Under normal conditions, GRP/SMC Panel Tanks have life of more than 50 years and Zinc-Aluminum Water Tanks have lifespan of more than 20 years. Both the tanks are modular and can be easily transported to most of the places and can be quickly installed by our expert installation teams in matter of few days. We can supply and install both these tanks in any parts across India and abroad.

Here are the details of each type of tanks:

GRP/SMC Panel Water Tanks

ERATOZ™ GRP/SMC Tanks are most advanced and practical solution for water storage. Our tanks are made with highest precision panels and components. We select all our components that surpass all requirements of Indian Standards IS:14399 Part 1 & 2, PSB Standards, BS standards, and all universal standards.

Zinc-Aluminum Water Tanks

We offer Premium Quality Zincalume / Zinc-Aluminum Pre-Engineered Water Storage Tanks for Industrial, Government, Rural and other Projects. As we make almost all parts of the tank in-house with state-of-art technology, our Zinc-Aluminum Water Tanks are of best quality as per international quality standards.
